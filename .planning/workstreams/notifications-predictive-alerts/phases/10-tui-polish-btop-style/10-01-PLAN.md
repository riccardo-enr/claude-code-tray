Give the usage panel its btop signals: a fixed three-band proximity color on each
cap's %, burn rate and reset countdown (TUI-06), and a per-cell gradient progress-bar
gauge for the 5h and 7d rows (TUI-07). The assertable halves -- the band cutoff logic

proven after this plan, before the trends and sessions visuals are built on the same
color approach.
